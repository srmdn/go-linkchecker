# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent; focus feedback on outcomes.
- Welcome different perspectives and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or abuse.
- Personal attacks or hateful conduct.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or project work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates this policy.
Repeated violations can lead to temporary or permanent bans.

## Reporting

Open a private security advisory on GitHub or contact the maintainer via the email listed in `SECURITY.md`.
